Why crypto escrow exchange?

Escrow services are the key to more secure payments. This financial arrangement allows a representative third party to bring neutrality to transactions by holding payment of the cryptocurrency required for two parties involved in a given transaction. The escrow service provider manages the cryptocurrency in question and keeps them secure throughout the duration of the transaction.

How Crypto Escrow Exchange Works

BaseSwap ships with most advanced technologies and easy to use tools for secure crypto exchange processes. We have highly experienced team of cryptocurrency officers. Our services ensure that the terms of the transaction agreed upon by both the Buyer and Seller are satisfied.

Create an escrow account to Start or join a Trade

One of the two parties, either the Buyer or Seller, will initiate the transaction. This process begins when the user inquires about the offering and creates an account on BaseSwap. With an account set up, the two parties can solidify the terms of their agreement.

Buyer and Seller pays on Baseswap

The Buyer or Seller can initiate the trade. The Buyer and Seller sends the cryptocurrency from their account to the baseswap.io escrow service, where it is immediately transferred to a USD stablecoin and stored in a no-interest escrow account. The value of the cryptocurrency is protected from volatility until both parties agree the transaction is complete.
We have provided multiple payment options to complete exchange payments, this includes connecting digital wallets like MetaMask and walletConnect supported wallets (like Trust Wallet) to Baseswap in other to securely complete and sign payments all at once.

Trade terms are reviewed and approved

Our systems review all trade terms to ensure both buyer and seller have satisfied all transaction terms for the crypto exchange. In case of any despute, our team have been highly trained to settle any despute that might arise during exchange. However this is unlikely to happen as our highly advanced systems ensure both parties automatically fulfills the exchange terms.

BaseSwap releases Crypto Funds

Once all trade terms are approved, the funds are immediately released to both seller's and buyer's specified wallet addresses. All you need do is click on withdraw, verify your account and you receive the funds in your wallet As soon as possible depending on the network at the given time.

BaseSwap Virtual Wallet

Create a wallet on baseswap.io, to hold cryptocurrencies as stable coins. You can transfer, withdraw, and Trade with wallet funds.
To use wallet funds for escrow exchange or to Buy and sell crypto, you must fund your wallet with the desired cryptocurrency. Wallet funds are not affected by market liquidity, so you can hold you crypto tokens on BaseSwap for as long as you want before joining or creating trades with them.
